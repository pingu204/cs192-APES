TERMS OF SERVICE

What to expect from us

Affiliations with UP Diliman and CRS

APES is an independent tool created to assist students at the University of the Philippines—Diliman (UPD) in efficiently organizing their schedules. While APES uses data from the Computerized Registration System (CRS) through its official API, it is not affiliated with or endorsed by UPD. APES simply integrates this data to help students build a timetable tailored to their preferences and constraints. Users should be aware that APES’s schedule recommendations are based on CRS data and do not guarantee enrollment in specific classes.

Limitations of the Software

APES aims to generate optimized schedules based on user preferences—such as earliest start time, latest end time, and specific class exclusions—while resolving scheduling conflicts as best as possible. However, APES does not ensure successful registration in any given class, as it cannot influence the CRS system’s allocation. Users should view the generated schedules as guides and refer to CRS for official course registration and final confirmation of class assignments.

Use License

APES is a web-based scheduling tool offered exclusively for UPD students to support their course planning. By using APES, users agree to access it solely for personal schedule management purposes. Redistribution, modification, or commercial use of APES software or data is prohibited. APES reserves the right to update its terms of use as needed, and continued use implies acceptance of these updates. The APES team may introduce new features or improvements, and users should review any changes to the terms regularly.

PRIVACY POLICY

What we do with your data

Personal Information

To provide personalized scheduling recommendations, APES may collect basic information such as your name, preferred courses, and scheduling preferences. This information is used exclusively to generate an optimal schedule for you and is not shared with third parties. Your personal information will only be retained as long as necessary for scheduling purposes and will be deleted or anonymized upon request.

Data Security

APES is committed to safeguarding the data of all UPD students. We employ secure storage and encryption protocols to protect user information and limit access to authorized personnel. The data APES retrieves from CRS is used strictly for generating schedules and is not stored beyond the user session, unless explicitly saved by the user. While we continuously update our security measures to prevent unauthorized access, please remember that no system is entirely immune to potential security risks.
