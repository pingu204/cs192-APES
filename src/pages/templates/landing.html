TERMS OF SERVICE

What to expect from us

Affiliations with UP Diliman and CRS

APES is an independent tool created to assist students at the University of the Philippines—Diliman (UPD) in efficiently organizing their schedules. While APES uses data from the Computerized Registration System (CRS) through its official API, it is not affiliated with or endorsed by UPD. APES simply integrates this data to help students build a timetable tailored to their preferences and constraints. Users should be aware that APES’s schedule recommendations are based on CRS data and do not guarantee enrollment in specific classes.

Limitations of the Software

APES aims to generate optimized schedules based on user preferences—such as earliest start time, latest end time, and specific class exclusions—while resolving scheduling conflicts as best as possible. However, APES does not ensure successful registration in any given class, as it cannot influence the CRS system’s allocation. Users should view the generated schedules as guides and refer to CRS for official course registration and final confirmation of class assignments.

APES is a web-based scheduling tool offered exclusively for UPD students to support their course planning. By using APES, users agree to access it solely for personal schedule management purposes.
